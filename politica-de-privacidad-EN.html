Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [13.07.2024]

Welcome to N’gruna. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or interact with us in other ways. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information

We collect personal information when you subscribe to our newsletter, contact us, or interact with us through our social media channels. The personal information we may collect includes:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Comments, questions, or requests made through our website or social media channels
  • Dietary preferences or special requests (if applicable)

1.2. Browsing Information

We collect information about your visit to our website, including:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type and Operating System
  • Pages Visited and Duration of Visit
  • Links Clicked

1.3. Social Media Information

If you interact with us via social media, we may collect public information from your profile, such as:

  • Username
  • Comments or messages sent to our page

2. Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, comments, and requests
  • Send you updates, offers, and promotions, if you have given your consent
  • Improve our service and your experience at our restaurant
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Protection of Personal Information

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Using encryption technologies to protect communications and transmitted information
  • Access control to restrict access to personal information to employees who need to know it to provide services
  • Regular audits and reviews of our privacy practices to ensure we are complying with our policies and applicable laws
