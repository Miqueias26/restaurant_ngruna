About Us

Welcome to N’gruna, a true gem of Mediterranean cuisine located in L'Estartit, a beautiful town in the Catalonia region of Spain. Since our founding in 1997, we have been dedicated to providing our guests with an exceptional culinary experience, combining the rich tradition of Mediterranean cooking with a touch of innovation.

Our Story

N’gruna began its journey in 1997, in the heart of L'Estartit. From the beginning, our goal has been to create a place where people can enjoy authentic Mediterranean cuisine in a cozy and relaxed atmosphere. Over the years, we have perfected our recipes and techniques, always using fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Our Specialty

We specialize in Mediterranean cuisine, a culinary tradition that celebrates the vibrant and fresh flavors of the sea and land. On our menu, you will find a variety of dishes showcasing the best of Mediterranean cooking, from fresh seafood to succulent meats and a wide selection of tapas.

The Chef

Josep Morillas is the owner and head chef of N’gruna. With an innate passion for cooking, Josep has dedicated his life to perfecting his craft and sharing his love for Mediterranean food with his guests. His creativity and attention to detail are reflected in every dish that comes out of our kitchen.

Our Location

We are located at Passeig Molinet, 5, 17258 L'Estartit, Girona. Our restaurant not only offers an unforgettable dining experience but also stunning views of the sea, creating the perfect setting for any occasion.

Come Visit Us

We invite you to visit us and enjoy a unique culinary experience at N’gruna. Whether it’s for a romantic dinner, a special celebration, or just a meal with friends, we are here to make your visit memorable.
