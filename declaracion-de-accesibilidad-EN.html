Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: [13.07.2024]

Commitment to Accessibility

At N’gruna Restaurant, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to comply with accessibility standards and provide a positive user experience for all visitors.

Measures to Support Accessibility

We have taken the following measures to ensure the accessibility of our website:

  • Incorporating accessibility as part of our organizational mission.
  • Integrating accessibility practices into our internal policies.
  • Providing ongoing training for our staff on web accessibility issues.
  • Conducting regular accessibility tests.

Conformity Status

Our website partially complies with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, due to the following exceptions:

  • Some older content may not be fully adapted.
  • There may be errors in some third-party documents.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please let us know.
